Who is OneRepublic?

OneRepublic consists of seven members: Ryan Tedder (Piano), Zach Filkins (Guitar), Jerrod Bettis (Sound), Drew Brown (Piano), Brent Kutzie (Guitar), Brian Willet (Percussion), and Eddie Fisher (Drums).

OneRepublic's Best Albums

  • Dreaming Out Loud(8.8): Boosted OneRepublic into the mainstream + "Apologize" is arguablly their best song ever produced.
  • Artificial Paradise(9.6): Creates the most nostolgic summer experience ever + "I Ain't Worried" and "West Coast" give the songs in Native a run for their money.
  • Native(10/10): The album that took over 2013...enough said.
